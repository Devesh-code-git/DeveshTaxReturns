Documents Required First time filer

Following documents required: (for first time filer and all other clients)

  1. T4 Slips from all employers
  2. T4A Slip if any
  3. T4E Slip if any
  4. Notice of Assessment
  5. RRSP/FHSA investment
  6. T5 Slip if any
  7. T5008 Slip if any
  8. T2202 for Tution fees
  9. Donation receipts if any
  10. Rent amount, number of months stayed, landlord name and adress (for Ontario & BC resident only) (as per CRA, rent receipt and lease required to claim rent for Ontario Trillium Benefit. CRA may ask you to provide proof in future)
  11. **For repeat filer, if your address, SIN number and/or martial status changed, please update
