Documents Required First time filer

Following information required for first time filer

  1. First name and surname
  2. Your date of birth
  3. Address
  4. Arrival date in Canada
  5. Marriage satus
  6. Spouse name if married
  7. If married, is your spouse in Canada?
  8. Spouse arrival data (if in Canada)
  9. Your Void Cheque / Bank Direct Deposit form
  10. Social Insurance number paper
  11. Your province of residence
